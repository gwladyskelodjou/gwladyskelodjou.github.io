Gwladys Kelodjou

I am a PhD student in computer science at the University of Rennes, conducting research on Explainable Artificial Intelligence with Alexandre Termier, Laurence Rozé, and Véronique Masson. I am part of the LACODAM Inria team at the IRISA Laboratory (France). My work aims to improve the quality of model decision explanations, making them more reliable, interpretable, and useful.

Keywords:
Explainable AI Interpretable ML Fairness Trustworthiness Robustness Stability

Education

Ph.D. Candidate

2022 - Now

University of Rennes, France

A key challenge in machine learning model explainability is the instability of existing explanation methods, where the same instance can yield significantly different explanations across queries. This thesis aims to develop approaches that provide consistent and robust explanations, improving both the interpretability and reliability of artificial intelligence models.
